KIA-SPORATAGE

The Sportage model belongs to the SUV category, equipped with a four-cylinder engine, 1,600 cc, producing 140 “horses” and a maximum torque of 16 Nm, connected to a six-speed “automatic” transmission. According to the data announced by Kia, the car accelerates from a standing position to 100 km / h, in 12.1 seconds. The average fuel consumption is about 6.7 liters, when traveling distances of up to 100 km. 2022

KIA-SORENTO

The Kia Sorento car comes in the Egyptian market to compete with cars such as the new Seat Tarraco, Peugeot 5008 and Skoda Kodiaq, and we also find that it competes with Toyota Fortuner and Grand Cherokee because they are a local assembly and with the same classification in different price categories. Kia Sorento is a Korean car of origin that is assembled locally in Egypt. The car is a medium-sized crossover. The car comes to the Egyptian market affiliated to the EIT Automotive Agency. The car is available in the market in three categories, its price starts from 550 thousand pounds to 750 thousand pounds. The car is a large family car, as it has seven seats.
